AAC Block Manufacturing Process

Product Description

Description of Autoclaved Aerated Concrete (AAC):

Johan Axel Ericksson,a Swedisharchitect,successfully combined these technologies and in 1924 developed the method for producing AAC. It is best choice material for Construction industry for Residential projects,Industrialprojects,Housing,Bunglows,Agricultural projects because of eco friendly and its different properties.



Raw Materials:

AAC is an eco friendly building material made from Fly Ash or Sand,Cement, Quick Lime powder,Aluminiumpowder,gypsum and water.Slurry made from Fly Ash and water poured with accurate weight as per formulation ,denstity required mix with cement and other raw material in pouring mixer to form aerated mixture .Further curing ,demoulding ,cutting,Autoclaving process form AAC blocks an insulating material with different properties.
